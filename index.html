Biography

Lin-Manuel Miranda was born on January 16, 1980 in New York City, New York, USA. He is an actor and composer, known for:

  • Hamilton (2020)
  • In the Heights (2021)
  • Mary Poppins Returns (2018)
  • And a LOT more
He has been married to Vanessa Nadal Miranda since September 5, 2010. They have two children.


Some of his creations

In The Heights

In The Heights is a musical Lin-Manuel Miranda created while thinking of his Latin roots. In the show, he plays Usnavi, the main character, which is eager to go back to the Dominican Republic, but at the end stays in Washington Heights, acknowledging it as his Home

Hamilton

Miranda played ALEXANDER HAMILTON in his show HAMILTON, which he was inspired by the Alexander Hamilton's biography he once read. For Miranda, Hamilton was all about rap.
